HTML (HyperText Markup Language) is the foundation of every webpage on the internet. Think of it as the skeleton that gives structure to your content!

HTML uses tags to tell the browser what each part of your content means. Tags are like labels that wrap around your content.
<h1>Hello World</h1>
Creates a large heading
<p>This is text</p>
Creates a paragraph
<img src="photo.jpg">
Displays an image
